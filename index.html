Search.  Post.  Win

With our new platform, any individual can post job offers from their current company and job seekers can apply through them. Candidates gain more trust and the chances to have an interview increase significantly, while posters can earn money from their employee referral program.

   Job Poster

Let's say your company has an open position and you find out they also offer as reward a considerable sum of money. Now is the perfect time to get in touch with people that might fit the job description using our platform.

   Job Seeker

For candidates, the main advantage represents the fact that employers tend to believe they’re better workers, because current employees should know who’s likely to be a good fit. As a result, they get faster reviews and responses, and vastly better hire rates.

How does it work

Employees post the Job offers

Job seekers contact the employee and sends resume

Employees refer candidates to HR through their Employee Referral Program

Candidates are contacted by HR

If selected, the poster gets the bonus from his company and optionally awards the candidate with a share from the reward
