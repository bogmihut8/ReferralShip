Welcome to ReferralShip, a platform on which employees can use their employer's referral program, post offers and get in contact with individuals that are looking for a job, generating a win-win-win situation. Take a good look, get used to the interface and start posting/seeking job offers right now!

  Find offers

Check out the offers list, get in touch with the author, discuss further about the vacant position, company or benefits and get your dream job.

  Create your offers

You can access your created offers right here, create more, edit or delete the existing ones. Hit "More details" to see a preview and make sure all the informations are correct.

  Edit your account

You can change your password or edit information like your name, birthday, gender, and phone number associated with your account from here.
